11/06/202410:00-12:00SeminarHow to teach Elephants to collaborate at Bending Spoons. The magic behind real-time collaboration in Evernote
Bending Spoons is a technology company based in Milan. Since its founding in 2013, the company has served over half a billion people across the globe through its suite of digital technology products, including Meetup, Remini, Splice, and Evernote. During this presentation, we will see how Bending Spoons implemented real-time editing on Evernote, from locating the problem to integrating the best solution.
