# Welcome to Giuseppe, our new PhD student!

We are excited to welcome **Giuseppe Arbore**, who joined the e-Lite research group as a PhD student on March 1, 2026!

[Giuseppe](https://www.polito.it/personale?p=giuseppe.arbore) is beginning his [PhD in Computer and Control Engineering](https://www.polito.it/en/education/phd-programmes-and-postgraduate-school/phd-programmes/computer-and-control-engineering) (41st cycle), supervised by Luigi De Russis with the collaboratio of Tommaso Calò from the IT University of Copenhagen.

Giuseppe has a background in software engineering and human-computer interaction, having recently completed his MSc in Computer Engineering at Politecnico di Torino. For his master's thesis, he developed _DigiZen_, an LLM-powered mobile app for digital wellbeing that goes beyond simple screen-time tracking, offering personalised recommendations, daily micro-goals, and motivational feedback to help young adults build healthier digital habits without imposing rigid restrictions.

In line with this work, Giuseppe's doctoral research focuses on human-centred AI, specifically on _how end users can meaningfully guide and personalise the behaviour of agentic interfaces_.

Welcome to the e-Lite group, Giuseppe! 🚀
