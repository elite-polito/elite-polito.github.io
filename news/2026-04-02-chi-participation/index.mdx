# Participation at CHI 2026

The e-Lite group will attend the _[ACM CHI Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems (CHI '26)](https://chi2026.acm.org/)_ on April 13-17, 2026, in Barcelona, Spain.

Luigi, Alberto, Luca, Francesca, Andrea S., Riccardo, Giuseppe, and Andrea D.L. will be there in person. If you see any of us, feel free to say hello: we're always happy to chat! 😊

The team will present two full papers (🏅), a TOCHI paper, six posters, and three workshop papers, along with organising a workshop and participating in the Student Mentoring Program.

Both full papers have received an __[honourable mention](/news/2026/03/23/chi2026-HM)__. Alberto is also one of the organisers of the [Bridge Over Troubled Water](https://chi2026.darkpatternsresearchandimpact.com/) workshop on aligning commercial incentives with ethical design practice to combat deceptive patterns. Finally, Luca will take part in the Student Mentoring Program, gaining mentorship from experienced researchers as he continues his PhD.
