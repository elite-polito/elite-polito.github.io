Please consider the following information as a _tentative preview_ of the course organization; it is provided only for helping students organize and plan, and is subject to change without notice.

Moreover, all lectures will be video-recorded and will be available on the Portale della Didattica, as well as here and in a dedicated [YouTube playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLs7DWGc_wmwSfM5xRFqwSr7vWQcl5VErw).
